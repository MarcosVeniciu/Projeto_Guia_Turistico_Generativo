Pousada Porto das Conchas offers a sauna and free private parking, and is within 8.1 km of Japanese Island and 8.5 km of Municipal Theater. A buffet breakfast is available every morning at the bed and breakfast. Guests can also relax in the garden. Surf Museum is 8.6 km from Pousada Porto das Conchas, while Water Square is 9.1 km from the property. The nearest airport is Cabo Frio International Airport, 13 km from the accommodation.
